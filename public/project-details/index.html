Addressing Disproportionate Gun Possession Charges among Young Black Men in Englewood

Tools: R (Tidyverse, ggplot2) Client: Teamwork Englewood Overview As Project Manager, I led a five-person volunteer team that analyzed Chicago crime data to identify trends in first-time gun possession cases, particularly looking at first-time gun possession cases among young Black men in Englewood. The project focused specifically on the experiences of young Black men in the Englewood neighborhood to support data-driven advocacy. I architected an analytical pipeline in R to load and integrate multiple datasets and generate time-series visualizations, surfacing critical trends in felony review and bond outcomes. These tools, in conjunction with policy materials produced by my teammates, directly support the client’s advocacy efforts following the elimination of cash bail. ...

Identifying Chicago Neighborhoods at Risk of Gentrification

Tools: ArcGIS Course: GIS Applications in the Social Sciences Overview Working with a teammate, we developed a spatial analysis model to identify Chicago census tracts most at risk of gentrification. Rather than relying on a single indicator, we implemented a composite indexing framework that synthesized market pressure, demographic change, and housing instability to flag high-risk areas. The resulting spatial tools enable policymakers to identify vulnerable zones and implement targeted housing interventions before displacement occurs. ...
