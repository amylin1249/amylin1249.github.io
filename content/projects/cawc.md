**Tools:** UX Design, Figma
**Client:** Connections for Abused Women and their Children (CAWC)

### Overview
As Project Lead, I spearheaded a ten-person volunteer team in a comprehensive digital redesign for a local nonprofit serving domestic violence survivors. I defined the site-wide information architecture, establishing the strategic vision for how content should be reorganized to streamline access to vital resources, and optimized the donor interface to improve conversion rates. We provided the organization with a modernized, trauma-informed digital strategy to better serve their community and scale their fundraising capacity.

### My Contributions
* **Navigational Overhaul:** Redesigned the site-wide hierarchy and internal linking to facilitate access to resources for users in crisis.
* **Conversion Optimization:** Streamlined the donor page interface to reduce friction and improve donation conversion rates.
* **Project Leadership:** Served as the primary liaison between the volunteer team and nonprofit leadership to align technical designs with organizational goals, authored the final documentation, and delivered a complete design package to the client’s leadership team, providing a ready-to-implement blueprint for their digital presence.
